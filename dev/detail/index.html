BasicBSpline.bsplinebasis — Method

B-spline basis function. Modified version.

\[\begin{aligned} {B}_{(i,p,k)}(t) &= \frac{t-k_{i}}{k_{i+p}-k_{i}}{B}_{(i,p-1,k)}(t) +\frac{k_{i+p+1}-t}{k_{i+p+1}-k_{i+1}}{B}_{(i+1,p-1,k)}(t) \\ {B}_{(i,0,k)}(t) &= \begin{cases} &1\quad (k_{i}\le t<k_{i+1}<k_{l})\\ &1\quad (k_{i}\le t\le k_{i+1}=k_{l})\\ &0\quad (\text{otherwise}) \end{cases} \end{aligned}\]
